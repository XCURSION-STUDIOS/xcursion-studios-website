Overview

The Xcursion identity needed to feel like the philosophy: unhurried, considered, willing to occupy space without filling it. Luxury editorial aesthetics without the distance — warm enough to be personal, refined enough to be taken seriously.

The system covers wordmark, typographic hierarchy, colour palette (amber and indigo in tension against deep black), motion principles, and surface applications across web and print.

Tools & Stack

Brand strategy and visual identity developed in Figma. Type choices locked to Cormorant Garamond and Cormorant SC for editorial display, DM Mono for utility and interface contexts.
