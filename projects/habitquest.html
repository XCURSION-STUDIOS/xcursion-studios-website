Overview

HabitQuest started from a simple frustration: every habit tracker feels like a productivity spreadsheet. They measure, they log, they remind — but they don’t make the thing you’re trying to build feel worth building.

The idea was to graft genuine RPG mechanics onto habit formation. Complete your daily habits, earn XP, level up your character, unlock quests. The habit loop stays the same; the reward system feels entirely different.

Tools & Stack

Built in React with Vite for fast development cycles. State managed locally for the MVP with a migration to persistent storage planned. Designed mobile-first throughout.
