Overview

Orbit is a personal exploration of spatial gradients as a compositional language. Each poster in the series treats colour as mass — objects with weight, distance, and gravitational pull on one another.

The brief was self-set: six posters, each using only two hues from the Xcursion palette, no typography beyond a single numeral, no ornamentation. Just colour, proportion, and the tension between them.

Tools & Stack

Designed entirely in Figma with custom noise and grain applied in post. Colour values were locked to the Xcursion brand system; everything else was free.
